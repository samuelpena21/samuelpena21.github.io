Privacy Policy

Fuel Tracker

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Fuel Tracker is developed and operated by Samp Dev ("we," "our," or "us").

This Privacy Policy explains how Fuel Tracker collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Fuel Tracker is designed to help users log and manage vehicle fuel refill data.

Information you provide

When using the app, you may enter information such as:

  • Odometer readings
  • Fuel quantity
  • Fuel price
  • Fuel type
  • Gas station name
  • Other fuel log details you choose to add

Advertising-related data

Fuel Tracker uses advertising services. Those services may collect certain data automatically, such as device identifiers, advertising data, app interaction data, and diagnostics, depending on how the ads SDK operates and how your app is configured.

Technical information

We may also process limited technical information necessary for app functionality, stability, and compatibility, such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Store and display your fuel log history
  • Help you track refill activity and related usage data
  • Maintain app functionality
  • Improve performance and stability
  • Display ads in the app

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Fuel log information you enter in Fuel Tracker is currently intended to be stored locally on your device, unless a future version adds cloud backup or sync functionality.

Advertising-related data may be collected or processed by third-party advertising providers as described in their own policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with third parties only in the following cases:

  • With advertising providers required to serve ads in the app
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the app, or others

5. Advertising

Fuel Tracker uses third-party advertising services, such as Google AdMob. These services may collect or process data such as advertising identifiers, device information, and app interaction information in order to provide, personalize, or measure ads, subject to their own policies and your implementation choices.

6. Children's Privacy

Fuel Tracker is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

7. Data Retention

Data you enter in Fuel Tracker may remain on your device until you delete it, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

Data collected by third-party advertising providers may be retained according to their own retention policies.

8. Your Choices and Data Deletion

You can delete your app data by:

  • Deleting entries within the app, if supported
  • Clearing the app's storage from your Android device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Because Fuel Tracker currently does not use user accounts, there is no account-based deletion process at this time. If a future version adds accounts or cloud sync, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by Fuel Tracker. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
