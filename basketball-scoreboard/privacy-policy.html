Privacy Policy

Basketball Scoreboard

Effective date: February 1, 2026

Overview

Basketball Scoreboard ("the App") is designed to work without collecting personal data from you. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App does (and does not) collect and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information from users.

  • No account creation is required.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs that collect data.
  • We do not sell or share personal data.

Information Processed on Your Device

The App may store and process certain information locally on your device to provide its features (for example: your scoreboard settings, game configuration, or preferences). This information is not transmitted to us and remains on your device.

Permissions

If the App requests device permissions (for example: notifications, Bluetooth, camera, photos, or location), it will use them only to provide the feature you requested and not to collect or send personal data to us.

You can control permissions at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party services that collect user data (such as analytics providers, advertising networks, or external crash reporting).

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not retain personal data on our servers. Any locally stored data (such as settings) remains on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's storage (depending on your device).

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how the App handles data, we will update this Privacy Policy and change the "Effective date" above.
